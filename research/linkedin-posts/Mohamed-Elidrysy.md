## Post 1: LinkedIn Content and Cold Outreach as One System
**Date collected:** June 2026
**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mohamedelidrysy_most-b2b-saas-founders-treat-linkedin-like-ugcPost-7461743446287011841-BlKS/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACjYR14B6Z6VdxZqkrxBVc3qcnhIVmTzIfA

**Summary:** Elidrysy argues that most B2B SaaS founders treat LinkedIn as an isolated marketing campaign, posting content and waiting for inbound results before abandoning it, similar to how they prematurely give up on cold email. His core argument is that LinkedIn functions as a trust-building layer rather than a direct response channel: every post acts as a small trust deposit with a founder's ICP, so that when cold outreach eventually reaches that same audience, the prospect no longer perceives the sender as a stranger. He claims this compounding familiarity improves outreach conversion rates, shortens sales cycles, and lowers cost per meeting over time, but only if content and outreach are run as a single integrated system rather than as separate efforts.

**Key takeaway for playbook:** Cold outreach performs better when paired with consistent LinkedIn content targeted at the same ICP, since prior visibility reduces the "stranger" friction outreach normally has to overcome. Content and outreach should be planned together, not as separate channels.
