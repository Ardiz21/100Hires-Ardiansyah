## Post 1: Cold Outreach Don'ts
**Date collected:** June 2026
**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jake-weber-b03625135_buildtosolve-share-7468794555539304448-v-6I/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACjYR14B6Z6VdxZqkrxBVc3qcnhIVmTzIfA

**Summary:** Weber lists common cold outreach mistakes that signal low effort or AI-generated content to prospects: generic closing lines like "worth a quick chat?", fabricated competitive-pressure stats in cold calls, and surface-level "personalization" that just feeds a prospect's LinkedIn post or website tagline into ChatGPT. He argues these tactics are easily detected and fall flat. His broader point is that outreach is inherently a volume-and-iteration game: reps should expect to be ignored often, treat each attempt as a test, and focus on extracting lessons from repetition rather than searching for one perfect script or sequence.

**Key takeaway for playbook:** Avoid generic AI-flavored closers and shallow personalization shortcuts; these are now widely recognized by prospects. Treat outreach as an iterative practice built on volume and learning from failed attempts, not a search for a single perfect template.
