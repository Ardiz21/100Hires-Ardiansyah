## Post 1: The MIC Method
**Date collected:** June 2026
**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/morganjingramamp_after-a-decade-in-outbound-i-think-i-finally-activity-7469742943440916480-6ipi?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACjYR14B6Z6VdxZqkrxBVc3qcnhIVmTzIfA

**Summary:** Ingram reflects on a decade in outbound sales and argues that AI has commoditized research and content, making reps sound increasingly similar. His response is the "MIC" framework: Magnetic (buyers feel genuine intent behind messages), Intuitive (reps can adapt when conversations go off-script), and Consistent (same energy and system applied daily, not just near quota deadlines). He backs this with examples of reps achieving strong meeting-booked and reply rates through social selling built on this approach. His broader claim: AI will keep improving and commoditizing research tasks, but human differentiation in outbound will increasingly come down to how reps personally show up, not what tools or scripts they use.

**Key takeaway for playbook:** As AI commoditizes research and list-building, the differentiator in cold outreach shifts to rep-level consistency and authentic engagement rather than content quality or tooling alone.
