# Alex Berman — LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1: Cold Email Myths — Targeting & Deliverability
**Date collected:** June 2026
**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexanderberman_cold-email-is-not-about-subject-lines-activity-7471916873882730499-lnO4?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACjYR14B6Z6VdxZqkrxBVc3qcnhIVmTzIfA

**Summary:** Berman argues cold email performance has little to do with subject lines or clever copy. He breaks it down into two real levers: targeting and deliverability. He shares a case where a client was filtering leads by company size instead of department size, causing mismatched outreach; fixing the targeting alone fixed response rates. On deliverability, he recommends moving away from Gmail/Outlook to dedicated SMTP infrastructure, spreading sends across multiple domains, and capping volume at roughly 2 emails per inbox per day to avoid spam filters.

**Key takeaway for playbook:** Reply rates are driven more by list quality and inbox infrastructure than by copywriting. Verify employment status, email validity, and job title accuracy before focusing on message wording.
